Mapping global hopes and fears to represent the unrepresented

The Dream proyect seeks to bringing understanding between humans, allowing people to share stories all around the world of the two things that shape our lives: our greatest aspirations and our worst fears. This project aims to be fluid, in hopes that it'll adapt to provide useful information for charities, NGO, governments, and corporations.
